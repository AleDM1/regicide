Quick rules

Defeat 12 enemies (4J → 4Q → 4K) by dealing damage with your cards.

  • ♥ Hearts – shuffle discard, place N cards under Tavern deck.
  • ♦ Diamonds – draw N cards from Tavern.
  • ♣ Clubs – damage counts double.
  • ♠ Spades – reduce enemy attack by N (cumulative).

Each enemy is immune to its own suit power (cards still deal damage).

Combos: play 2–4 cards of the same rank with total value ≤ 10.
Animal Companions (Ace): can pair with any single non-Jester card.
Jester token (Solo): discard hand, draw 8 fresh cards (2 per game).

After playing, the enemy attacks you — discard cards totalling ≥ enemy ATK.
If you can't cover the damage, you lose.

Defeat last King → Gold (0 Jesters) / Silver (1) / Bronze (2).
